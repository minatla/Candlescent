CREATE YOUR PERFECT SCENT

Candlescent is known for our at-home custom candle-making experience that allows you to create your own candle from your choice of over 20 different fragrance oils.

“Nothing brings to life again a forgotten memory like a fragrance.”
-Christopher Poindexter
1. Choose your scent
Choose 3 fragrences from over 20 different options. Our fragrances range from earthy & masculine to floral & fresh. Don't know which scents to choose from? Purchase a sample kit and select your favorite scents.
2. Select your vessel
We have three sizes available for candles. The number of wicks will vary with the size.
3. Enjoy your candle
After customizing your candle, our team of candle specialists will create your custom product. Once it is ready, we will send it out to you to enjoy!

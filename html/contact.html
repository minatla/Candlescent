About Us

Welcome to Candlescent! We provide resources for you to customize your own candle from the comfort of your own home! Making custom fragrance products is a unique and enjoyable activity that is perfect for groups and individuals of all ages. Our fragrances range from earthy and masculine to floral and fruity. We also provide some other unique options including foods and beverages like Champagne and Baked Bread. We offer over 20 unique, premium fragrance oils to choose and design your own custom scent to be blended into a candle.
